Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how InvoiceHub, developed by TorqBrain, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access and use the application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following categories of information:

- Account information, such as name, email address, and authentication details provided through Supabase Auth or Google Sign-In.
- Business information, such as company name, address, tax identifiers, bank details, invoice numbering preferences, signature text, and branding details.
- Customer information, such as customer names, contact details, addresses, and tax information entered by users.
- Product and inventory information, such as product names, SKUs, prices, quantities, stock adjustments, and business mappings.
- Invoice information, such as invoice numbers, invoice dates, due dates, line items, amounts, notes, payment status, and received amounts.
- Technical information, such as browser type, device information, IP address, usage activity, and application error logs where available.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- provide and operate the InvoiceHub application;
- authenticate users and secure access to data;
- create, manage, preview, and store invoices;
- maintain business, customer, product, and inventory records;
- improve app performance, usability, and reliability;
- respond to support requests and user inquiries;
- comply with legal obligations where required.

3. Data Ownership

Users retain ownership of the business and customer data they enter into the application. TorqBrain does not claim ownership over invoice, customer, business, or inventory records created by users.

4. Data Storage and Processing

Application data may be processed and stored using third-party infrastructure and services, including Supabase and hosting providers such as Netlify. By using the application, users acknowledge that their data may be processed through these service providers.

5. Authentication and Security

We use authentication and access-control measures intended to prevent unauthorized access. While reasonable efforts are made to protect information, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Users are responsible for:

- maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials;
- limiting access to their devices and accounts;
- promptly notifying us if they believe their account has been compromised.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only:

- with infrastructure and service providers needed to operate the application;
- when required by law, regulation, or lawful request;
- to protect the rights, property, or safety of users, TorqBrain, or the public.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, unless deletion is requested and legally permitted.

8. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring they have appropriate rights and legal basis to enter and process customer, invoice, employee, or tax-related data within the application.

9. Third-Party Services

The application may rely on third-party services for hosting, authentication, storage, analytics, and infrastructure. Their handling of data is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

The application is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date.
